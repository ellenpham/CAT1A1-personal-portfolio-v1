This is me.

I am Ellen Pham - a full-stack developer, based in Melbourne, Australia. I am always fascinated about how technology has evolved and how amazing applications and programs are created to serve us in daily life. That is why I start coding, I want to be a part of the tech industry, contribute to create real programs that solve real-world problems. Currently a boot-camper at Coder Academy, I strive to expand my tech knowledge and sharpen my coding skills. I always put in 100% effort in all of my projects. Back in 2017, I worked in marketing data management team at Bosch, Singapore. In 2019, I moved back to Australia and worked as a resourcing specialist at Programmed Skilled Workforce. My love for technology and the confidence working with systems gradually grew throughout years of performing database management and data analysis. Yet, I still found the job was not challenging enough. In 2020, I had my first child. Being a mother gives me the strength and motivation to build myself a career that is flexible enough to achieve work-life balance.

I then took my courage and leapt into the new found passion - coding. I love combining the worlds of logic and creative design to make engaging, functional and accessible websites and applications. Above all, I strive to deliver meaningful storytelling through digital experience.

Here I quoted my favorite saying from Maya Angelou. Switching to tech career as a female/mother is not easy but I believe that as long as you put your heart in whatever you do, work hard and never give up, you will get rewarded with fruitful outcome.

I truly hope one day I can inspire and encourage young mothers to take the leap and get educated in this fast-changing world of technology. And that one day my kids could proudly say that "My mom is a coder".

"Do the best you can until you know better. Then when you know better, do better."

"It is literally true that you can succeed best and quickest by helping others to succeed."

Me, when I am not coding, might be not like any coders you might think of. I am a yoga lover, a plants addict, an aspiring chef and a spiritual enthusiast. I am mindful in everything I do and find my happiness in the givings. I bring those mindset into my projects and aim for human-centered digital products that serve and connect to users.

That also makes me a team-oriented person, I communicate and collaborate with peers with dedication and respect. I keep an open mind when it comes to reaching out for assistance and clarifying my questions. By myself, I learn and work diligently to support the team achieve common goals.
